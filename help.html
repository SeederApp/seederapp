Introduction
Welcome to Seeder, a social platform designed for BlackBerry developers and users. Users, help the Blackberry community grow by submitting app ideas you want to see made. Developers, look through the list of proposed ideas for inspiration and start building apps you know are in demand.
Together we can enrich BlackBerry. Together we can plant the seeds of tomorrow.
Terminology
Enrich:
Show your support for an app idea by enriching it. Apps can be sorted by popularity or by date. Each app can be enriched only once.
Seed:
A seed is quite simply an app idea. A seed's popularity is based on how many times it's been enriched by the community.
Planting:
Developers show their interest in building an app by planting a seed. Any seed planted by a developer will be decorated to show who's currently working on it.
Comments:
Seeder is a social platform so we encourage open discussions around each seed. Thought of a way to improve an idea? Let the creator know!
Report:
Naughty seeds can be reported with ease. After accumulating enough reports, these naughty seeds will cease to appear with the rest.
